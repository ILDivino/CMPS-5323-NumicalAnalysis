$\zeta_{A,B} = \sum_{i=1}^{N}(A*B) - N * \bar{A} * \bar{B}$  
$\beta = \zeta_{x,y} / \zeta_{x,x}$  
$m' = \beta$  
$A = e^{\bar{y} - \beta * \bar{x}}$  
$b' = \bar{y} - \beta * \bar{x}$
